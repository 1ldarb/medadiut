Workflow

From dictation to structured record
in five effortless steps

Designed around the doctor's natural workflow — not around software.

1

Doctor Dictates

The physician speaks or types notes naturally — in Hebrew, English, or a mix. No special formatting required.

2

On-Site Anonymisation

Patient names, ID numbers (ת.ז.), and contact details are removed on your server before processing begins.

3

Smart Clinical Structuring

AI identifies symptoms, duration, medications, allergies and preliminary diagnosis — and formats them precisely.

4

Automated Protocol Verification

Your clinic's internal treatment protocols are automatically cross-referenced to support best-practice documentation.

5

Hebrew RTL Report

A polished, right-to-left formatted clinical note is ready — copy directly into your EMR or print for the chart.

Privacy & security

Built for the strictest
medical data standards

MedAdiut was designed from the ground up to satisfy the Israeli Ministry of Health data privacy directives — not just to comply, but to exceed them.

On-premises PII redaction. Names, phone numbers, Israeli ID numbers and addresses are stripped locally — before any AI processing occurs.
No patient PII sent to any external server. The AI processes only anonymised clinical content.
Automatic Teudat Zehut detection. Israeli 9-digit ID patterns are caught and redacted instantly, even if staff forget.
Full audit trail. Every document event is logged locally for compliance review.
